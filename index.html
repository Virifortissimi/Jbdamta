3 SIGNS THAT YOUR FUEL MAY BE CONTAMINATED

Compromised fuel can cause serious damage to your fuel tank and degrade the operation of your equipment. That’s why it’s important to be aware of the condition of your fuel tanks. You may need fuel tank decontamination services if you notice:

  • Murky or dark fuel samples
  • Unusual-colored smoke
  • Fuel tank damage

If you suspect that your fuel has been contaminated by water, microbes or debris, call for a fuel tank cleaning service from JB Damta.
